The Story

I've spent over sixteen years making images lie beautifully — retouching for the world's biggest brands, compositing impossible scenes frame by frame. Then AI arrived, and everything changed. I didn't resist it. I absorbed it. Today, I merge decades of pixel-level discipline with generative AI, 3D environments, and code. I build things — commercials, apps, websites, visual systems — that shouldn't exist yet, but do.
